About Me

I am Sk Aslam Ali, an Embedded Systems Developer with a background in Electronics and Communication Engineering. I specialize in working with ESP32, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and various industrial embedded systems. My expertise includes smart displays, GPS geofencing technology, RS485 automation systems, and more.

I have developed projects ranging from small wearables to home automation systems and GPRS geofencing. My passion lies in creating innovative solutions that bridge the gap between hardware and software.
