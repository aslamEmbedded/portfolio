Microcontroller Configurations

ESP32

240 MHz dual-core, 520KB SRAM, WiFi + Bluetooth, 34 GPIOs, ADC, DAC, I2C, SPI, PWM.

STM32F103

72 MHz ARM Cortex-M3, 20KB SRAM, 37 GPIOs, multiple timers, UART, ADC, SPI, I2C, USB.

ESP8266

80 MHz single-core, 64KB instruction RAM, WiFi, 17 GPIOs, SPI, I2C, UART, great for IoT AP mode.

Arduino Uno

16 MHz ATmega328P, 2KB SRAM, 14 digital I/O, 6 analog inputs, I2C, SPI, UART, beginner-friendly.
